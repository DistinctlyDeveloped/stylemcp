Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a request?

Each API call to /api/validate, /api/rewrite, or /api/validate/batch counts as one request, regardless of text length. Batch requests count based on the number of items in the batch.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference. When downgrading, the change takes effect at the next billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my limit?

API requests will return a 429 error until your quota resets on the 1st of each month. You can upgrade anytime to get more requests immediately.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes, contact us for annual billing options. Annual plans come with 2 months free (pay for 10, get 12).

Can I self-host StyleMCP?

Enterprise customers can self-host StyleMCP on their own infrastructure. Contact sales for licensing details.

Is there a free trial?

The Free tier is essentially a permanent trial. For Pro and Team features, contact us for a 14-day trial with full access.
